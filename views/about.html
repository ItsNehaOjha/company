About Us

Swarashtra News is a premier independent digital media newsroom for North India, dedicated to local governance, policy transparency, and community narratives.

Company Introduction

Founded in the heart of Uttar Pradesh, Swarashtra News has emerged as a leading voice of journalism across North India. We are an independent digital-first news organization, providing detailed ground reports, investigations, political updates, and business analyses that shape our regional and national progress.

Unlike traditional networks, we concentrate on stories that impact daily lives, bringing municipal decisions, administrative accountability, agricultural challenges, and grassroots policy implications directly to our readers. Our team of experienced reporters, analysts, and editors maintain the highest standards of investigative and ethical journalism.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission: To empower the citizens of India by providing accurate, non-partisan, and verified news. We aim to protect democratic values by questioning those in power and amplifying local issues that are often overlooked by national media.

Our Vision: To become India’s most trusted digital news ecosystem, bridging the information gap between state policy formulation and grassroots implementation. We envision a society shaped by facts, constructive debates, and ethical civic participation.

Core Values

Truth & Integrity

We deliver facts exactly as they are. Sourcing transparency is non-negotiable in our newsroom.

Neutrality

We remain objective and free of political, commercial, or corporate influence.

Public Accountability

We act as the fourth pillar of society, holding public servants and leaders accountable to the populace.

Community Focus

We prioritize local interests, highlighting regional struggles, triumphs, and development reports.

Editorial Standards

Every piece of journalism published on Swarashtra goes through a multi-stage editing and verification process. We confirm reports with multiple primary sources, cross-check legal and financial documents, and ensure that editorial opinions are clearly demarcated from objective reporting.

We strictly adhere to the Guidelines of the Press Council of India and respect ethical codes of privacy, factuality, and fair representation.

Technology & Innovation

At Swarashtra, we believe that modern journalism must be powered by modern technology. Our website utilizes high-speed static delivery, secure SQLite databases, and structured metadata configurations to ensure pages load instantaneously even on slow mobile networks common in rural areas.

Our RSS aggregator continuously scans trusted news sources to build a robust index of regional happenings, allowing our news desk to immediately verify and report on breaking alerts in local communities.

Why Trust Us?

Trust is earned through transparency. We clearly credit our writers, link to primary documents and resources, and promptly publish corrections when errors are brought to our attention. Our business model relies on independent readers, preventing political advertisers or corporate backers from dictating our coverage. When you read Swarashtra, you read the unfiltered truth.
