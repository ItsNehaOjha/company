# Swarashtra Corporate Assets Management

This directory serves as the centralized repository for all corporate, brand, and media assets for Swarashtra News. Having a unified assets structure ensures brand consistency across print, digital, and legal operations.

## Directory Structure & Purpose

- **`logos/`**: Contains the official Swarashtra logo in high-resolution vector format (SVG, EPS, AI) and raster formats (PNG, JPG) for both light and dark backgrounds.
- **`stamps/`**: Holds official digital rubber stamps, verification marks, and editorial seals used to authenticate legal papers and major reports.
- **`signatures/`**: Houses authorized signature graphics of directors, editors, and the official HTML email signatures (e.g., `email_signature.html`) used in professional email clients.
- **`letterheads/`**: Stores corporate letterhead templates in PDF and Word formats for formal communications, partnerships, and editorial notices.
- **`favicons/`**: Dedicated to browser tab favicons and mobile app shortcut icons in various dimensions (PNG, ICO, WebP).
- **`social/`**: Contains pre-formatted graphics for social platforms, including Twitter/X headers, Facebook cover photos, and Instagram/YouTube branding layouts.
- **`documents/`**: Holds official corporate policies, certificates of incorporation, tax registration papers (GST), and standard partnership agreement PDFs.

*Note: Do not store random editorial images or article assets here. Use this directory strictly for official company-level brand assets.*
